We do not directly collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) from users of the App.
However, third-party advertising networks and mediation platforms (such as Appodeal) integrated into the App may collect certain data from your device, including but not limited to:

  • Advertising ID (e.g., Google Advertising ID)

This data is collected to serve personalized or non-personalized ads and to analyze app usage.
We do not have access to, nor do we control, the data collected by these networks. For more details, please review the privacy policies of the advertising partners used in our App.

These networks may collect certain device or usage data for the purpose of personalized and non-personalized ads (see section above).

For more information, please refer to their respective privacy policies:

  • Appodeal Privacy Policy

3. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe your child has provided personal data without your consent, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such data.

4. Data Security

While we do not directly collect or store personal data, we recommend users keep their device and account secure.
